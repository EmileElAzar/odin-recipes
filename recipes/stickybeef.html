Sticky Beef Recipe

The image above is what you are going to be cooking!!

Ingredients (to serve 4 people):

  • 3 cups of rice
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1kg of minced beef
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 2 onions
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the bell peppers lengthwise and deseed.
  2. Cut the onions lengthwise.
  3. Add the rice and the water to one big pot of water.
  4. Salt the water and cook at high heat for 10 - 15 minutes.
  5. Put the beef in a pan and cook until browned.
  6. Add the vegetables and cook through for 2 - 5 mins.
  7. Remove the beef and vegetables and cover to avoid cooling.
  8. Add soy and hoisin sauce to a pan with oil and ratio at taste.
  9. When the sauce thickens, add the beef and vegetables and cook through for 2 mins.
  10. Remove, serve on rice and enjoy!
