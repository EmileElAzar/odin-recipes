Eggs on Toast Recipe

We will learn how to prepare this!

Ingredients for two toasts with eggs:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of toast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • 1 Avocado

Steps

  1. Cut the avocado lengthwise and remove the core.
  2. Put oil in a pan and raise to medium heat.
  3. When ready, crack two eggs into the pan and put the toast into a toaster.
  4. Season in pan.
  5. When toast is done, put avocado on them.
  6. When eggs are done, put them on the toast from the pan.
