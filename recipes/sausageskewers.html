Sausage Skewers Recipe

We will learn how to prepare this!

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 4 Wooden Skewers
  • 4 Potatoes
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 4 Honey Mustard Sausages
  • 200ml Sweet & Sticky BBQ Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 C
  2. Slice the potatoes lengthwise, season and drizzle with oil and put them in the oven.
  3. Halve the pepper, remove the seeds and chop into wedges.
  4. Chop the onions into small wedges.
  5. Slice the sausages into small chunks.
  6. Put peppers, onions and sausages into a bowl and add BBQ.
  7. Mix together thoroughly.
  8. Thread the veggies and sausage onto skewers: Sausage-Pepper-Onion order.
  9. Put skewers onto baking paper and put into the oven. Cook for 20-25 mins.
  10. When sausage skewers are cooked, remove and serve with potatoes.
